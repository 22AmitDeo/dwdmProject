1. PRICE DISTRIBUTION HISTOGRAM
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE:
Show how frequently certain price ranges occur and overall variability in pricing

VISUALIZATION TYPE:
4-panel histogram showing distributions of:
  1. Modal Price Distribution
  2. Volatility Distribution
  3. Minimum Price Distribution
  4. Maximum Price Distribution

KEY FEATURES:
  • 50 bins for detailed frequency analysis
  • Mean and median lines for reference
  • Color-coded for easy interpretation
  • Grid for precise reading

INSIGHTS PROVIDED:
  • Price concentration ranges
  • Skewness in price distribution
  • Outlier identification
  • Market price variability
  • Common price points

USE CASES:
  • Understanding market price structure
  • Identifying price anomalies
  • Policy pricing decisions
  • Market efficiency assessment

OUTPUT FILE:
  01_price_distribution_histogram.png
